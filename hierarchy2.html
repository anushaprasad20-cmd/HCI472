Discipline in a single family. Here, hierarchy is earned not by reaching for a different typeface but by reaching into the one already chosen — weight, width, case, italic. Nine grades of Archivo, three width positions, one grid. The Swiss instinct applied to a contemporary grotesque.

WEIGHT IS STRUCTURE WHEN ONLY ONE FAMILY SPEAKS.

At 900-weight and 120-width, Archivo pushes into display territory without asking a second typeface for help. Uppercase keeps the line geometric and architectural. Read the notes on variable axes below.

Condensed width as hierarchical tool

H3 narrows the width axis to 85 — a quieter, more efficient voice. Condensed widths are useful exactly here: between display and running text, where density reads as authority.

The smallest heading in the system.

At H4 the width opens back up to 110 and the weight steps to 600, giving a clear separation from body copy without crowding the section heads above. A measured grade, not a loud one.

05
Body Text 16 px
1.250 ⁰
Weight 400
Width 100
Leading 1.65

Body text is the reason the hierarchy exists. Archivo at 400 weight and default width sets into long paragraphs with composure — open counters, even color, a slight mechanical quality that is at home on the screen. The measure holds at sixty-two characters, the leading at 1.65, and nothing else changes. When one family carries the entire page, consistency at the body level is what makes every other level legible as a deviation.

06

italic
Weight 700
Width 115

Emphasis combines three axis moves at once — italic, a step in weight, a step in width. No color shift. The triple move makes emphasized words feel intentional within an otherwise monochromatic page, without reaching for a different family or a foreign hue. This is the efficiency argument for a single-typeface system: every distinction is already available inside the file.

07
Link Text 16 px
Weight 600
Width 100
1.5px underline

Links are the one concession to color — a single signal red, reserved for interactive text. A half-step weight increase and a firm 1.5-pixel underline hold the link visible in a paragraph of 400-weight text. Hover inverts the relationship: the link becomes a block of color, which is the clearest possible confirmation that the cursor is in the right place.

Modular Scale

1.250 — Major Third.

Each size is multiplied by 1.25, a gentler ratio than the Perfect Fourth. The smaller jumps suit a single-typeface system: with no change of family to mark hierarchy, large size gaps would feel arbitrary. Here, the modest multiplier leaves room for weight and width to do the structural work.
